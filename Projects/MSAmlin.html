MS Amlin Insurance strengthens its position in the Dutch Garage-specific niche market.

Only a few insurers in The Netherlands offer the Garage Insurance product, due to its complex risks (e.g. hail damage, theft) that need specific Insurers' expertise. The Global Insurance Company MS Amlin Insurance has strong expertise in complex Garage risks and prevention solutions. Yet, low visibility. MS Amlin also offers Mechanisation Insurance.
