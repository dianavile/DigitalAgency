MS Amlin Insurance

MS Amlin Insurance has strong expertise in complex Garage risks and prevention solutions. Only a few insurers in The Netherlands offer the Garage Insurance product, due to its complex risks (e.g. hail damage, theft) that need specific Insurers' expertise. MS Amlin also offers Mechanisation Insurance.
