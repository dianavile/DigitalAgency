Proyecte Equal Landingpage

Proyecte Equal is a project of Initiativa Barcelona Open Data(iBod) to investigate how Catalan youngsters (16-23 years) visualize and see sexual violence "machismo" and equality receive in their Instagram feed.

Goal: Create an Awareness Campaign on Instagram to visualize sexual violence and equality in Instagram feed. Publish the results of the data-analysis study on a Landingpage. And share best practices from Gender experts to improve equality and decrease sexual violence on Instagram in Catalonia.
