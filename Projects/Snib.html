SNIB

SNIB stands for Stichting Nederlands in Barcelona. This Dutch school in Barcelona teaches the Dutch language and culture for children aged 4-16 on Saturdays.

The Challenge

What started as a voluntary movement from some parents to learn their kids Dutch back in 2013, grow into what it is now. A Dutch School recognized by the Dutch Government (NOB). This school prepares Dutch children abroad with at least one Dutch parent for the official certificate CNAV, Dutch as a second language.

SNIB celebrated its 10-year anniversary in 2023. With the aim of professionalizing the school, the organization asked for an online presence. Here is the result.
